The Mission

Grind2Give is more than a gaming community — it's a movement that turns every stream, every match, every grind into real, measurable good for those in need. United in the grind, we turn pixels into plates, lobbies into lunch, streams into second chances, and the least of these into the seen. Grind’s hard. Mission’s harder. That’s why we need you.

Here's how it works:

  • Community votes monthly on which charity receives the next donation pool (your voice decides)
  • Live donations sent transparently via The Giving Block — receipts and on-chain proof shown on stream
  • Passive rewards for supporters who hold and participate
  • Full transparency: public wallets, on-chain tracking, no hidden fees

This is what Matthew 25:40 looks like in action — grinding together to serve the least of these.
