Our Mission

Grind Hard. Give Harder.

At the heart of Grind2Give ($G2G) is a single, timeless truth straight from the words of Jesus Himself:

“Whatever you did for one of the least of these brothers and sisters of mine, you did for me.” — Matthew 25:40

We believe that every late-night gaming session, every hard-fought PvP match, every strategic trade, and every community celebration can become an act of worship and compassion.

While most cryptocurrencies exist only to chase price charts and most charity coins deliver one-time donations with little transparency, Grind2Give is fundamentally different.

We are not another speculative token.
We are not a temporary feel-good project.
We are a faith-driven, game-powered movement that turns digital grind into real-world good — automatically, transparently, and perpetually.

Every time $G2G is bought, sold, or transferred:

  • 0% Buy Tax — Zero friction when entering the ecosystem.
  • 5% Sell Tax and 3% Transfer Tax — A very small and fair fee split between charity, reflections, and burn.

This means your grind literally feeds the hungry, clothes the naked, visits the sick, and comforts the imprisoned — exactly as Matthew 25 commands.

What makes us truly unique:

  • PvP-P2E Gaming at the core — Strike2Give Bowling (currently in development) along with multiple future competitive games.
  • Community decides the impact — Monthly voting on charity recipients.
  • Radical transparency — All donations via The Giving Block with public proof.
  • Built-in investor protections — Cooldown + max wallet + max tx limits.

Grind2Give is the bridge between the gaming world and the Kingdom mandate. This is Matthew 25 in blockchain form.
