Gaming Grind → Real-World Good • March 2026

Abstract

Grind2Give ($G2G) V2 is a community-first ERC-20 utility token on Base that turns gaming streams, participation, and trading into real charitable impact.

With a clean 0% buy / 5% sell tax, every sell automatically generates ETH reflections for holders and sends funds to community-voted charities.

Charity Mechanism

2.5% of every sell is automatically sent to the Charity Multisig Wallet. The community votes monthly on the next charity recipient via X (Twitter) polls. Donations are sent through The Giving Block and all donation proofs are publicly posted on X, Telegram, and Facebook.

Security & Future Plans

• Smart contract has not been audited yet. An independent audit is planned in the near future.
• Ownership will be renounced after game logic and core features are fully completed and tested.
