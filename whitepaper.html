Abstract

Grind2Give ($G2G) V2 is a community-first ERC-20 utility token on Base that turns gaming streams, participation, and trading into real charitable impact.

With a clean 0% buy / 5% sell tax, every sell automatically generates ETH reflections for holders and sends funds to community-voted charities — proving that the grind can feed the hungry, clothe the naked, and lift up the least of these.

Inspired by Matthew 25:40, Grind2Give is more than a token — it's a movement where pixels become plates, lobbies become lunch, and streams become second chances.

Detailed Allocation Explanations

LP Lock — 78.75%
100% of the initial liquidity pool is locked for 3 years via PinkSale. This is the strongest possible trust signal — fully verifiable on-chain and eliminating any risk of rug pulls while ensuring long-term market stability.

Burn (Dead Wallet) — 5.00%
An initial 5% of the total supply was permanently burned at launch. This creates immediate deflationary pressure and demonstrates strong commitment to holders from day one.

Dev / Mission Support — 4.32%
A transparent wallet used for ongoing development, operations, platform improvements, and advancing the charitable mission. This wallet earns ETH reflections just like every other holder, ensuring full alignment with the community.

Airdrops — 5.91%
Dedicated to community growth, onboarding new players, giveaways, stream rewards, and viral adoption campaigns — directly fueling the Grind2Give movement.

Telegram Mini-App Rewards — 3.94%
The core rewards pool that powers the in-game economy inside the Grind2Give Telegram Mini-App. Players earn $G2G by grinding, competing, and participating — turning playtime into real-world charitable impact.

Future Reserve (New Pairs) — 0.50%
A small strategic reserve to seed additional liquidity pairs (e.g., USDC, DAI, or other stables) as the project scales and new trading opportunities arise.

Remaining / Ops Buffer — 2.09%
The natural leftover supply after LP creation and initial allocations. This flexible buffer supports day-to-day operations and minor initiatives without diluting the core tokenomics.

Tokenomics Highlights

  • ~84% of supply is either permanently locked, burned, or allocated to community rewards and growth.
  • Modest dev allocation with full reflection participation — no hidden advantages.
  • Built-in deflation through the initial burn + ongoing 0.5% auto-burn on sells.
  • One of the strongest 3-year LP locks in the Base ecosystem.

Roadmap

Phase 1: Preparation & Deploy — March 23rd, 2026

Finalize all v2 configurations and deploy the upgraded contract on 20Lab.

Phase 2: Migration — March 29th, 2026

Snapshot v1 holders and manually distribute a 1:1 airdrop of new G2G tokens to verified wallets. Post batch proofs on X and announce migration completion.

Phase 3: Launch & Activation — March 24th, 2026

Publish the official launch announcement with the new contract address and LP lock link. Begin small airdrops and mini-app reward distributions. Highlight minor fixes and improvements as they roll out.

Phase 4: Telegram Mini App Beta — April 10th, 2026

Launch the Telegram mini-app P2P/P2E beta. Players can battle, earn rewards, and stress-test the economy before full release.

Phase 5: Telegram Mini App Full Version — April 17th, 2026

Release the full Telegram mini-app with polished P2P/P2E gameplay, a stable economy, and a complete feature set following beta testing.

Phase 6a: Remove Restrictions — April 24th, 2026

All remaining limits are lifted once daily volume reaches $10K. Protections stay in place until then to shield the community from snipers and whales, ensuring a fair and stable early market.

Phase 6b: Renounce Ownership — April 24th, 2026

Ownership is renounced after Phase 4 is completed and the ecosystem is stable. This final step completes decentralization and ensures the contract becomes fully trustless and community-owned.

Phase 7: Growth and Long Term Goals — May 1st, 2026

Ongoing growth driven by the team and community. Continued support, ecosystem updates, and long-term commitment to expanding impact, strengthening the mission, and sustaining the charity pipeline.
