Abstract

Grind2Give ($G2G) is a community-driven ERC-20 utility token on the Base blockchain that transforms gaming streams, chat participation and grinds into verifiable charitable impact.

Through an asymmetric 1% buy / 8% sell / 0% transfer tax structure, every public trade automatically contributes to causes chosen by the community — turning playtime into purpose.

Inspired by Matthew 25:40 — “Truly I tell you, whatever you did for one of the least of these brothers and sisters of mine, you did for me” — Grind2Give exists to prove that collective gaming hustle can feed the hungry, equip kids with sports opportunities, support families in crisis, and lift up the overlooked.

Launch Process

  1. Deploy token on Base — full supply to founder wallet
  2. Burn 5% to dead address
  3. Fund public wallets (airdrops 4.5%, founder 4%, community engagement staff 1%, project operations staff 1%, future reserve 0.5%)
  4. Renounce ownership + verify contract on Basescan
  5. Pre-launch airdrops from rewards wallet to build hype
  6. Add liquidity live on stream (initial ETH/WETH + 85% supply) on Aerodrome
  7. Lock LP tokens for 3 years (36 months) via UNCX or PinkSale — proof shared live

Community & Promotion

  • Streams: Any game — airdrops tied to participation (lobby joins, winners, chat activity) on twitch.tv/grind2give
  • Onboarding: Simple guides for Phantom Wallet → Base network → add $G2G token
  • Transparency: Live Basescan overlays, wallet balances, donation receipts

Charitable Execution (Community-Voted via The Giving Block)

  • Tax auto-sends to Safe multisig charity pool wallet
  • Monthly community votes on charities (Twitch/X polls)
  • Funds forwarded live via The Giving Block widget (receipts + on-chain proof shared)
  • Random Acts of Kindness (from Founder & Mission Support Wallet): Live-stream spontaneous help
