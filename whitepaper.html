Gaming Grind → Real-World Good

$G2G on Base • Version 1.0 – January 2026

Abstract

Grind2Give ($G2G) is a community-driven ERC-20 utility token on the Base blockchain that transforms gaming streams and participation into verifiable charitable impact.

Through a simple 6% transaction tax, every buy, sell, or transfer automatically contributes to causes chosen by the community — turning playtime into purpose.

Inspired by Matthew 25:40 — “Truly I tell you, whatever you did for one of the least of these brothers and sisters of mine, you did for me” — Grind2Give is built on the belief that collective gaming hustle can create lasting good for the overlooked and underserved.

1. Vision & Mission

The Problem

Gaming communities generate massive energy, time, and economic activity — yet most of it remains trapped in entertainment without touching real-world need. At the same time, millions face hunger, lack access to sports, live in crisis, and feel invisible.

The Solution

Grind2Give turns that energy outward. Every stream, every game, every trade becomes a vehicle for good. The 6% transaction tax is the engine:

  • 3% → Community-voted charity (monthly polls → forwarded live via The Giving Block)
  • 2% → Reflections (passive rewards to all holders)
  • 0.5% → Auto-burn (supply shrinks over time)
  • 0.5% → Auto-Liquidity (pool grows automatically)

Community votes ensure funds support causes that matter most — feeding the hungry, enabling youth sports, helping families in crisis — while holders earn passively and the ecosystem becomes more stable and scarce.

Core Values

  • Transparency — public wallets, on-chain proof, live receipts
  • Fairness — low founder allocation, renounce ownership, community control
  • Impact over speculation — real donations > hype
  • Inclusivity — anyone can participate (watch, hold, vote, give)

4. Launch Process

  1. Deploy token on Base — full supply to founder wallet
  2. Burn 5% to dead address
  3. Fund public wallets (airdrops 5%, founder 5%, community engagement 2%, project operations 2%, future reserve 1%)
  4. Renounce ownership + verify contract on Basescan
  5. Pre-launch airdrops from rewards wallet to build hype
  6. Add liquidity live on stream ($2,000 + 80% supply)
  7. Lock LP tokens for 3 years (36 months) via UNCX or PinkSale — proof shared live

5. Community & Promotion

  • Streams: Any game — airdrops tied to participation (lobby joins, winners, chat activity)
  • Onboarding: Simple guides for Trust Wallet / Coinbase Wallet → Base network → add $G2G token
  • Transparency: Live Basescan overlays, wallet balances, donation receipts

6. Charitable Execution (Community-Voted via The Giving Block)

  • 3% tax auto-sends to public charity pool wallet(s)
  • Monthly community votes on charities (Twitch/X polls)
  • Founder forwards funds live via The Giving Block widget (receipts + on-chain proof shared)
  • Random Acts of Kindness (from Founder & Mission Support Wallet): Live-stream spontaneous help

7. Risks & Disclaimers

  • No financial advice — $G2G is a utility/community token, not an investment. Value may go to zero.
  • Volatility — Crypto markets are unpredictable.
  • Tax — Token transactions are not charitable donations. Direct donations to qualified charities may be tax-deductible (consult a tax professional).
  • Legal — Fair launch, no securities promises.
